Building Minetest on Sailfish
=============================

irrlicht/ogl-es available at:
http://sourceforge.net/p/irrlicht/code/HEAD/tree/branches/

cd build-arm
sb2 cmake .. -DIRRLICHT_INCLUDE_DIR=../../irrlicht-git/include \
	-DIRRLICHT_LIBRARY=../../irrlicht-git/lib/Linux/libIrrlicht.a \
	-DBUILD_SERVER=0 -DRUN_IN_PLACE=1 -DTARGET_SAILFISH=1
sb2 make -j2
